Top three activities to do at Bandung

Al-Jabbar Mosque

Al-Jabbar Mosque is a mosque located in the city of Bandung, West Java, Indonesia. The mosque is located in the city center, precisely in the area of Jalan Asia Afrika, and adjacent to Gedung Merdeka.

Bridge Pasopati

Pasupati Bridge is a bridge located in Bandung, West Java, Indonesia that connects the city of Bandung with the city of Cimahi. The bridge is located in the city center, precisely in the area of Jalan Asia Afrika, and adjacent to Gedung Merdeka.

Scenery of Bandung

Bandung is the capital city of West Java province in Indonesia. Much of the city is surrounded by hilly mountains, with the towering Tangkuban Perahu volcano north of the city offering sweeping views over the lush surrounds.
